# ContractorOS Pilot RFQ Request Pack

Use this request pack to obtain the missing evidence needed to close the deck
and plumbing controlled-estimating pilots. Send only the relevant section with
the project drawing and scope. Supplier and contractor responses must quote
each requested item separately; a lump-sum reply is not sufficient for seed
validation.

## Deck contractor quote

Please price installation separately from materials and state:

- crew roles and day rates;
- expected duration and m2 output under the stated conditions;
- site access, carry distance, height, slope, cutting, fixing, waste, and
  rework assumptions;
- inclusions and exclusions for foundations/supports, engineer inspections,
  balustrade/interfaces, demolition, make-good, lifting, and transport;
- raw threaded-bar cutting method, 180mm and 230mm cut yields, kerf, offcuts,
  and labour cost per cut;
- VAT, validity period, insurance, warranties, and programme constraints.

## Plumbing installer quote

Please return a priced labour build-up, not a single installation allowance.
State the registered plumber, assistant/supervisor roles, hours per fixture,
testing and commissioning hours, certificate requirements, and exclusions for
reticulation, builder's work, electrical work, wall closure, finishes, and
transport. Identify all scope assumptions against the drawing and fixture
schedule.

## Acceptance rule

A response is admissible as pilot evidence only if it identifies the project,
date, quote number, supplier or contractor, units, VAT treatment, validity,
and stated inclusions/exclusions. Record admissible responses in
`pilot-evidence-register.csv` and reconcile them before any rate is promoted.
